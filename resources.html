Resources

Welcome to the F.F.F. resources page.


18 years old or younger?
If so, check out Summer Meals. There are over 800 summer meal sites in Oregon. They provide free meals for kids and teens 1-18. Go to the Summer Meal Site Finder to locate the nearest location for you.

Looking for fruits?
Look at Falling Fruit This website helps to "map the urban harvest" by locating all of the places near you where you can pick your own fruit for free. When you hover your mouse over an indicated location it will indicate what type of fruit is offered

Over 18 and or simply looking for more than fruit?
Try the Oregon Food Bank. At this webpage you will be able to enter your zipcode and see all of the locations, where and when, food is served.
