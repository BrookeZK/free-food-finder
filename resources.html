Resources

Welcome to the F.F.F. resources page.

18 years old or younger?
If so, check out Summer Meals.
There are over 800 summer
meal sites in Oregon. They provide
free meals for kids and teens 1-18.
Go to the Summer Meal Site Finder
to locate the nearest location for you.

Looking for fruits?
Try Falling FruitThis website helps to
"map the urban harvest"
by locating all of the places near you
where you can pick your own
fruit for free. When you hover your mouse
over a marked location it will indicate
what type of fruit is offered

Over 18 and or simply
looking for more than fruit?
Try the Oregon Food Bank.
At this webpage you will be able
to enter your zipcode
and see all of the locations,
where and when, food is served.

Looking more broadly
for government food
assistance programs?
Follow this link to look at the
Multnomah County "Get Food Guide.
At this website you will find
many more resources for food.

Not sure how to get there?
Checkout Trimet Trip Planner
to find the best route of public
transit to get you from
where you are
to where you want to be.
